# ⚔️ About Me

Biologist | Banker since 2005 | Data Analytics & AI student

Curious builder of tools powered by **code, automation and artificial intelligence**.

For two years I ran and worked in a **computer repair and hardware store**, gaining hands-on experience with hardware troubleshooting, operating systems and customer support.

In recent years I started studying programming, beginning with **Python**, during the rise of modern AI tools. My goal was simple: understand how things work, run models locally and build tools that solve real problems in my daily life.

Today I explore:

- Data Analysis
- Data Science
- AI-powered tools
- Automation
- Practical software solutions

Married and father of two amazing kids.
